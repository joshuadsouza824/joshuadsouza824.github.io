About Me

My name is Joshua Malcolm D.S. D’souza, I am 22 years old, and I was born on 4 April 2000
in the city of Ilagan, Isabela, Philippines to a Filipina mother and an Indian father.When I was six months old my parents
took to me Dubai, UAE where both were working. I spent my nursery and kindergarten years in Dubai. On January 1, 2006,
my father met a fatal motorbike accident. Sadly, he did not survive the accident and passed away.My father’s death
took a toll on the whole family and our lives have never been the same since then. My mother, being a single mother and sole
breadwinner of the family has left with no other choice but to send us back to Philippines to make ends meet. I was moved
to Philippines when I was six years old. I studied in Manila till I was in grade 4 at Lani Cassipe learning Centre and then
continued my studies at MECAP in Ilagan, Isabela. I transferred to Case Del Nino from grade 7 until I completed the 12th year
in high school. My mother works hard and saves money in order for me, my sister and my grandparents could come to Dubai and
other places for vacation during our school breaks. I am currently studying at Isabela State University taking up a Bachelor of
Information and Technology. My first two years in college was conducted online due to the pandemic. My mother asked me to stay
with her from December 2020 since our classes are being held online. At present, I am still in Dubai, UAE. I am a friendly person
yet shy sometimes. I am very passionate about playing basketball and building Gundam. I keep myself fit and healthy through regular
exercise in the Gym, playing basketball on weekends and swimming three times in a week. I like watching anime and playing video games.
I enjoy listening to music and I am crazy about slow rock music. I love watching basketball games and I am an avid fan of
Lebron James. Despite the availability of movies on TV and Netflix, I still prefer to watch movies at big screens, and I am
so glad that the cinemas have been opened in Dubai since January 2021. I am diligent and I take a conscious effort to achieve
a balanced healthy life. I manage my time properly to ensure I complete my assignments on time and spend enough time
to do my hobbies and other interests. I stay connected with family and friends through social media like Facebook and Instagram.
I am determined to work hard and achieve my goals in life. I will endeavor to graduate in college and get a good job so I can
support my mother who will eventually retire in few years’ times.My dream is to have a successful life and a happy family
someday. I am God fearing person and I have a high respect to people who are elder than me. My mother always tells me to
stay focused and do my best in all the things I do so I can succeed in life. I will always remember her advice to me and
make sure to practice and apply them appropriately as I go on with my life. I firmly believe in the saying “The key to success
is to focus on goals not obstacles”.This will serve as my inspirational quote throughout my journey in this world.
