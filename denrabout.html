About us



The Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) is responsible for the
conservation, management, and development of the country's environment and
natural resources. It shall ensure the proper use of these resources and the protection
of the environment within the framework of sustainable development.
It administers several concerns related to the management of various ranges of natural
resources in the Philippines such as land classification, forest inventory watershed
protection, biodiversity, water and mineral resources conservation
